---

### âœ… 3. **Default Values**

---

### âœ… 4. **Swapping Variables**


---

### âœ… 5. **Rest in Arrays**

---

### âœ… 6. **Nested Array Destructuring**

```js

```

---

### âœ… 7. **Destructuring in Function Parameters**
